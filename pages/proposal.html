Our proposal: The safety concerns of self-driving cars

Why did we choose this topic?

The advancement of AI accelerated the deployment of autonomous cars. However, numerous issues arose when adopting this technology into our life. One of the most concerning issues is the safety of autonomous cars. Despite the fact that big tech companies are investing into developing autonomous cars, fatalities due to autonomous cars still have occurred, and killed pedestrians. They are mainly caused by the car's failure to sense objects by its machine learning program or poor system deployment coded by humans (Baecker, 2019). Hence, we know it is not easy to deploy autonomous cars that are perfectly safe.

We believe autonomous cars require careful consideration of traffic controls to improve safety concerns. If autonomous cars are successful in carrying out safe operations, the impact on society will be immeasurable. We believe it will improve everyone’s lifestyle and help to avoid the fatality of driver-caused car accidents. Therefore, our team will investigate this problem and propose how our society should adopt autonomous cars safely.

How are we managing our work as a group?

We chose to use a Github repository for our website as it is a good way of collaborating with each other and ensuring that everyone can see the same version of the website. It also provides hosting for the website, for easy testing/viewing.

Github also provides a discussion board which could potentially be useful in discussing things about the website.
